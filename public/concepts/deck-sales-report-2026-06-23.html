The fix · speed-to-lead + a fixed cadence

A first-response and follow-up sequence is the highest-leverage system you don't have yet.

Right now new leads hit the estimator and wait. Three came in Jun 22 with no call, five voicemails sit unheard. A lead contacted in minutes books far more than one called the next day, and most leads need 5 or more touches before they quote. Make the sequence automatic so no lead goes cold by accident.

Touch 1 · minutes
Instant
New estimator lead fires an auto-text and email in minutes, plus a task to call the same day.
Touch 2-3 · day 1-2
Reach
Call again, text, email. Different channel each time until you get a human or a booked inspection.
Touch 4-6 · day 4-10
Persist
Value drops (10 Mistakes, Revive, proof photos) spaced out, every one ending in a clear next step.
Then
Quote
Inspection booked, proposal sent, into the proposal follow-up track. No lead exits without a number.
