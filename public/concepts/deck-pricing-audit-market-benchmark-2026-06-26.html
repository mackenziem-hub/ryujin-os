Gutters

Gutters are a margin strength. Keep them bundled into the roof.

Your $20/LF retail is premium against the $8-15/LF standalone market, but fully defensible as a roof-job add-on, a gutter-only shop cannot match that pricing power. Cost stack: chop-drop material ~$3-6/LF plus the $8/LF sub-hang = ~$11-14/LF against $20 retail.

Only caveat: a price-sensitive customer cross-shopping a gutter-only installer will see $20 as high. Keep it bundled and positioned as part of the roof system.
