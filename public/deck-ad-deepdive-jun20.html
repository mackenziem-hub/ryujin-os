Google diagnosis · why it produced nothing

Buying the wrong searches with a broken scoreboard.

01

Half the search terms are non-buyers. Roughly 55% of paid terms were freebie, charity or off-topic: "free roof replacement," "low income roofing assistance," "senior roof replacement program," "$99 a month no credit check," "roofing contractor cerca de mi," even "eavestrough" and "construction contractors." Phrase-match close variants are leaking the budget.

02

Conversion tracking is both broken and untrustworthy. Zero events have ever fired. Structurally there are two "Submit Lead Form" actions both set primary (a double-count waiting to happen), GA4 events are hidden, and the opt-in lead is excluded from the metric. With no signal, the bidding optimizes blind.

03

It barely shows up. 11.9% impression share means ~88% of available searches were missed even on the terms it did want. Underfunded and unfocused at the same time.

High-intent local search ("roofing companies Moncton," "metal roof Moncton") is genuinely valuable and Meta cannot replicate it. The problem is not Google, it is this setup. Do not kill the channel forever, rebuild it deliberately.
