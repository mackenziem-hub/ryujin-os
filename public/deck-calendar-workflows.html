What she changed

The build fixed a real bug and made every reschedule trackable.

  • Tracked links created trigger links for all 3 calendars and wired them into every reschedule and follow-up CTA, email and SMS, so clicks are measurable instead of blind custom values.
  • Copy consultation rewritten to first-person with a new graphic; inspection kept inspection-based; Revive kept on rejuvenation-fit.
  • Templates 8 branded email templates cloned and organized per calendar (customer-facing, internal notifications, no-show / canceled).
