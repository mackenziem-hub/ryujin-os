The Premise

What this sequence is for

When a homeowner uses the Instant Estimator and gets a price range, they're warm but not hot. They've shown intent (they cared enough to plug in their address and roof size), but they haven't booked an inspection. Most contractors blast them with 5 hard-sell emails over a week and burn the relationship. Plus Ultra doesn't.

The job

Either book the inspection, or earn permission to keep the conversation going. Either is a win. Pushing harder than that is how you lose them.

Day 2

A quick roofing tip most homeowners don't know

Email
Subject: A quick roofing tip most homeowners don't know
Hey {first_name}, Quick one. When people ask me what's the single biggest thing that kills a roof early, it's not the shingles. It's the ventilation. If your attic doesn't breathe properly, heat builds up in the summer and cooks the underside of the deck. In the winter, warm air from the house hits the cold roof, melts snow, and refreezes at the eaves. That's where ice dams come from. And that's how a 30-year shingle becomes a 15-year shingle. A good install isn't just laying the new material. It's checking the intake (soffit vents) and exhaust (ridge or roof vents), making sure the math works out, and fixing it if it doesn't. When we come out for the inspection, that's one of the first things we check. If you ever want to set that up, just reply or text 506-540-1052. Mackenzie

Branches

Engagement and booking paths

Engagement branch

After each email step, add an If/Else condition:

  • If email opened OR link clicked OR reply received → tag ie-engaged → pause workflow → notify Cat: "IE lead {first_name} {last_name} engaged. Take over manually."
  • Else continue to next touch

SMS replies are handled separately by EA-01's "Customer Replied" trigger, which notifies Cat. The nurture workflow doesn't need a duplicate SMS-engagement branch.

Booking branch · the win path

If at any point the contact's pipeline stage moves to Qualified, Inspection Booked:

  1. Apply tag ie-booked
  2. Stop the workflow (no more nurture touches)
  3. Send internal notification: "IE lead {first_name} converted to booked inspection. Nurture stopped."

This is the canonical "win" path. Track count weekly.
