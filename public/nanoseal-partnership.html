Section 03 · Community Campaign

Save A Roof Moncton — community giveaway concept

Save A Roof Moncton · Pilot Campaign
A free GoNano rejuvenation for a deserving Greater Moncton home
Plus Ultra owns the audience and the install. NanoSeal donates the product. Local-press worthy.
The pitch in one line A community-focused contest where Greater Moncton residents nominate a deserving homeowner whose roof Plus Ultra and NanoSeal will rejuvenate free. Both brands get a feel-good story with local-press legs.

Why this works

  • Mutual exposure at low cash cost. NanoSeal donates GoNano Revive. Plus Ultra donates application labor. Total combined cost = product-cost-only, not retail.
  • Earned media. A genuine community story is news-worthy in NB. Times & Transcript and CBC NB cover this. Free press > paid ads on credibility per dollar.
  • Audience growth. Nominations submitted via Plus Ultra landing page with email + phone capture. Non-winners enter the pipeline — both parties get the lead list.
  • Real testimonial pipeline. Winner becomes a case study: before/after photos, video walkthrough, voice testimonial. Reusable in all future marketing for both brands.
  • Cause-aligned with Plus Ultra brand. Plus Ultra donated $10K+ in 2024 sponsorships (East Coast Kyokushin Kenbukai, Hospital Activity Book for Children). This fits the existing brand narrative.

Mechanics

Eligibility

  • Homeowner residence in Greater Moncton (Riverview, Dieppe, Moncton, Salisbury, Coverdale)
  • Single-family home (no rentals, commercial, or investors)
  • Roof visually inspected and qualifies for Revive treatment (passes Ben's score sheet)
  • Roof at least 4 years old
  • Nominator and nominee can be the same person OR different (community nomination)

Submission process

  1. Visit plusultraroofing.com/save-a-roof
  2. Fill the form: nominee name, address, why they're deserving (200-500 words), 2-3 photos of the roof
  3. Email + phone for both nominator and nominee
  4. Consent for media + property access if selected

Judging

  • Plus Ultra + NanoSeal joint panel
  • Criteria: financial need / story (50%), eligibility per score sheet (30%), community impact / story quality (20%)
  • 1 winner selected, 2 runners-up offered discounted rejuvenation as consolation

Timeline

  • Week 1-2: Build landing page, set up email-collection funnel, draft media announcement, internal review
  • Week 3: Launch — paid Meta ads, organic FB, NanoSeal social, press release
  • Week 4-6: Open submission window
  • Week 7: Judging + reveal video filming
  • Week 8: Application + media coverage
  • Week 9-12: Case study content production, testimonial video, before / after social posts

Channels

  • Plus Ultra Meta organic (3 posts during open window)
  • Plus Ultra Meta paid ($500-1,000 boost)
  • NanoSeal social (cross-post)
  • Local press release (Times & Transcript, CBC NB, Acadie Nouvelle)
  • Plus Ultra blog post (SEO: "free roof rejuvenation Moncton")
  • Plus Ultra email list (announcement + reminder)
  • Yard sign at winner's property (post-application, co-branded)
