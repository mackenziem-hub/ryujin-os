Straight answers

Questions homeowners ask us

Why is Plus Ultra more than some other quotes?
A few things differ between contractors that do not always show in the price. The underlayment, ice and water versus felt. The shingle line, architectural versus builder grade. And how long the manufacturer warranty actually runs, full coverage versus shingle only. Ask any roofer those three questions and you will see exactly where the difference is.
What happens if you find rotten wood under the old roof?
The first 2 sheets of decking are included in your price. If we find more, we photograph it, show you, and you approve the $85 a sheet before we replace anything. You decide, with the evidence in front of you.
How long does the job take?
Most roofs your size are a one to two day job, weather permitting. We drop materials the day before, introduce the crew the morning of, send photo updates through the day, and finish with a full cleanup and magnet sweep for nails.
What if it leaks after you are done?
You are covered. The leak-free year-one guarantee is in your contract, and the workmanship warranty runs 15 to 25 years depending on your level. If it leaks because of our install, we fix it on our dime.
Can I finance it?
Yes, through FinanceIt, with no payments and no interest for the first 3 months. A soft credit check takes two minutes and does not affect your score. There is no penalty for paying it off early. Use the calculator above for a monthly estimate.
