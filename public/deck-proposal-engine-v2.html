Why this exists

Five proposal surfaces, all drifting

Before v2, a Plus Ultra proposal could be built five different ways, and every one of them drifted from the others.

Tier-card pages, the envelope configurator, the data-driven client page, gutter and add-on one-offs, and a hand-coded static HTML page whenever a deal needed a shape the builder could not model. Each surface carried its own copy and its own pricing seam.

03 / 12
The core idea

Offer = Sections + Products

Every proposal is the same two things. Compose them and you can express any shape.

Sections, the story

An ordered list pulled from a reusable library: hero, intro, proof, guarantee, comparison, before and after, scope, accept. Written once, shared by every proposal.

Products, the price

One stateful block that renders a single price, good / better / best, two priced paths, the configurator, gutters, or a repair, all from the same data.

The payoff

Five surfaces collapse into one renderer. New shapes are new data, not new pages.

04 / 12
Architecture

Four tables, one renderer

proposal_blocks holds the Sections library. proposal_templates holds the blueprints. proposal_instances stores each frozen, sent proposal. tenant_settings.proposal_v2_enabled gates the rollout.

Migrations 089 to 092, live in prod. One assembler, assembleProposalData, feeds the single proposal-v2.html renderer.

08 / 12
What this unlocks

Rejuvenation vs Replacement, finally data-driven

The old envelope builder could only stack shell upgrades onto one roof. It could not show two real either-or paths.

So a Revive versus Replace deal used to fall back to a hand-coded static page, splitting the proposal off the system. In v2 it is the rejuvenation-vs-replacement template: two priced paths, one renderer, no bespoke HTML.

The win

Repair plus Revive as an alternative to reroofing, the most common Plus Ultra case, is now a template instead of a one-off.

10 / 12
How a proposal goes live

Compose, preview, freeze

  1. Pick the estimate and a template.
  2. Compose the Sections and Products, edit copy live.
  3. Generate: the proposal freezes into an immutable snapshot.
  4. The customer link routes to /p/slug, and the old share link redirects to it.

No edits to a sent proposal

Once generated, the stored snapshot never changes. The guarantee is structural, not a promise.
