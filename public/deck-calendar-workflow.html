Layer 2 · Structure

A round-robin that plugs in both crews

The inspection calendar becomes a GHL Round Robin team calendar with two members: AJ and Diego. GHL distributes bookings between them and offers only slots that are actually open.

Prerequisite, and it is Cat's task

Connect each of AJ and Diego's calendars to GHL. That link is what lets round-robin check their real day and avoid double-booking. This is the "make AJ and Diego's calendars ready" item. Without it, round-robin books over them.

The backfill crew stays out of it

The Monday trial hire is production, not inspections. His purpose is to free AJ and Diego up, so the round-robin stays just the two of them.

Layer 3 · Routing

One smart calendar beats four, for now

Add a service-type field to the booking form and drive the slot length off it. Covers Cat's options 1 through 4 without building four calendars on day one.

Split into separate calendars only if one type starts crowding the others out. Revisit in a month.
