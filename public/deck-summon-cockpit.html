02 / 07
State 01
Idle

It waits, calm.

The home screen is the dragon, one input, and today's pulse in a few chips.

No wall of numbers. Nothing demanding attention. Just the question, what do you need? The chips are the only data on screen, and they are the few things that actually need you right now.

03 / 07
State 02
Listening

You speak. It focuses.

Type it or say it. The dragon lifts its head.

As you state intent, "schedule Cornhill," the chips recede and the agent narrows in. This is the moment the old way, open the app, click Production, hunt through tiles, collapses into a single line.

04 / 07
State 03
Summoning

The dragon surfaces. The view forms.

The agent does the cross-referencing you used to do by hand.

It pulls the job, checks the calendar, finds the open crew slot, and materializes the one object you need. A beat of motion, then the answer. This is the work happening, made visible.

05 / 07
State 04
One object, next move staged

One object. The next move ready.

The summoned job, and the single action that matters, pre-staged.

Confirm Thursday 7am, or adjust. The "Go?" is the agent's real approval step: it batches the writes and asks once. One object on screen, one decision to make, zero hunting.

06 / 07
State 05
Density is opt-in

Need the controls? Advanced expands in place.

The same cockpit, three depths: Agent, Interactive, Advanced.

Flip to Advanced and the full tab rail appears, Jobs, Schedule, Work Orders, clean and organized. You opt into density when you want it. It is never the default, and it never follows you home.
