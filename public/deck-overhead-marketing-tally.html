The catch the bank parse missed

Cat costs more than you think, and she is invisible in your Canadian books.

  • Why hidden She invoices $1,650 USD/month (invoice MM001) and you pay her through Wise. It posts as a currency transfer, not a "Catherine" line, so a pure CAD-statement parse drops her entirely.
  • Real cost At the ~1.47 exchange rate in your statements, that is ~$2,425 CAD/month, not the $2,000 you carry.
  • By design The extra is back pay. She has been working more and been more available than first planned, and you are happy paying for it. Not an error, a good problem.
  • Classification She is admin, not crew. Counted as overhead here. Your real crew payroll runs separately through the Intuit PADs and is job cost, not overhead.
Settled Cat carried at ~$2,425/mo, intentional. The only watch-out is that as a USD line she scales with the exchange rate, so a weaker CAD quietly raises this.
