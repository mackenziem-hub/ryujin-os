The doctrine in one picture

Cold to signed, channel by channel

Each channel has one job. When a channel does two jobs, the data goes blurry and the budget gets lazy.

Stage 1 · awarenessBrand top-of-mindMeta video views, awareness PMax. Optimize for hold + thru-play, not leads.
Stage 2 · intentCapture searchers + scrollersGoogle Ads on roofing keywords. Meta Leads on direct-CTA creative.
Stage 3 · quotedBooking + estimateInspections funnel, Instant Estimator, 10CM nurture sequence.
Stage 4 · signedCustomer lock-inProposal · contract · deposit · production. Out of marketing's hands.

Why the map matters

Every ad, every magnet, every nurture has to slot into exactly one stage. The "we've been getting-" video at $648 spend with zero leads is not a leads ad — it's a Stage 1 awareness ad, and judging it on CPL is the wrong test.

Lead magnets · what each one is for

Three magnets, three jobs. Don't mix them.

  • 10 Costly Mistakes is a list-builder, not a sales engine. April audit verdict: $38 historical CPL but the leads it produces convert at near-zero into quoted deals. Use it to fill the nurture queue. Do NOT spend more chasing more 10CM PDF grabs hoping they become roofs.
  • Instant Estimator is the high-intent conversion path. It quietly closed Mark Arzaga ($16K) and most signs of life on Plus Ultra's website come through it. IE-1 Leads Ad at $26 CPL is the channel feeder. Treat IE submissions as warm by default and speed-to-lead them within an hour.
  • Inspections funnel (booking.plusultraroofing.com/optin2026) is the direct booking path. Both Google Ads and Meta Leads feed it. Conversion tracking is now live on both sides as of Jun 7. This is the cleanest signal in the whole stack — protect it.

The follow-up gap

The April audit's hardest finding was that lead-to-quote conversion was a nurture failure, not a creative failure: 6-step Jewels script existed, only 1-2 texts actually went out. Today GHL handles the cadence automatically and the speed-to-lead path is mostly there — but the Al Kizzy IE lead from last night is sitting unanswered as I type this slide. The leak is still real.
