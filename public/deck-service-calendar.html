01
Why this matters

Three things break the flow today

🔍

You cannot reliably get to it

Asking Ryujin for the calendar can land you on a dead page. The right calendar should be one ask, every time.

↗️

Clicking a job throws you to another page

You just wanted to fix the assigned crew. Instead you leave the calendar, and there is no easy way back to where you were.

🚫

Nothing slides or stretches

Rescheduling means digging into a job record. There is no grab-and-drop, and no way to drag a job out across the days it will actually take.

The fix in one line

Make the calendar the workspace: edit in place, drag to move, drag to set duration. Never lose your spot.

02
Interaction 1

Pop it open. Fix it. Stay put.

Tap any booked job and a job card opens right on the calendar, over a dimmed grid. The page never changes.

Reassign the crew with one tap, Plus Ultra or Atlantic Roofing. Nudge the time. Drop a note for the crew. Hit Save and the card closes back onto the exact spot you were looking at.

Solves

"I just wanted to fix the assigned crew, but it brought me to a different page."

03
Interaction 2

Slide a job to a new day

Grab a job and drag it. A ghost of the block follows your cursor, the target day lights up, and a tag confirms where it will land before you let go.

Drop it. The booking moves. No forms, no page changes, no hunting for a date field.

Note

Moves write back to the booking source. Where a job lives in GHL, the drag updates GHL so the calendar and the booking stay in sync.

04
Interaction 3

Stretch it across the days it takes

A two-day tear-off is not a single dot on a calendar. Grab the edge of a job and drag it across the days. It becomes a span bar with a clear day count.

Now the week reads true: you can see at a glance which crews are tied up, and for how long, before you book the next one.

Solves

"It would be great to slide and move the jobs over and set them for an amount of days."

05
The view

Cleaner, color-coded, crew-aware

🌐

Week, Month, By Crew

One toggle. The same jobs, the lens you need: a packed week, a month overview, or a lane per crew.

🎯

Color tells the story

Inspections in teal, installs in gold, blocked time muted. You read the week in a glance.

👤

Crew on every job

The assigned crew shows right on the block, so reassigning is an exception you can spot, not a record you open.

06
One ask away

Ryujin always knows the way here

"Open the service calendar" should land you on the real calendar, every time, with zero dead ends.

🧮

The brain routes true

The cockpit's navigation is pinned to the real calendar instead of guessing a URL. No more landing on an empty page.

↩️

Back means back

If a job does open a full record, "Back" returns you to the calendar you came from, not a generic home screen.

07
How we ship it

Three phases, each usable on its own

PHASE 1 · FAST

Edit in place

  • Tap a job, open the inline card
  • Reassign crew + time + notes, Save
  • Pin Ryujin's "calendar" route to the real page
  • Smart Back returns to the calendar
PHASE 2 · THE MAGIC

Drag to move + span

  • Grab-and-drop to reschedule
  • Drag the edge to set duration
  • Writes sync back to GHL bookings
  • Conflict + double-book warnings
PHASE 3 · POLISH

Lenses + crews

  • By Crew lane view
  • Month overview refresh
  • Color legend + filters
  • Mobile drag tuning

One ground rule

GHL stays the source of truth for bookings. The calendar reads from it and, for drag edits, writes back to it, so nothing drifts out of sync.

Mockups are concept renders to lock the look and feel. Branding, exact labels, and crew names are illustrative and finalized in build.
