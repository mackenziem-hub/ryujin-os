What NuRoof Revive does

The science in three steps.

NuRoof Revive™ is a water-borne polymer rejuvenator. It penetrates aging asphalt shingles, restores the natural oils that UV strips out, and seals the surface against future moisture and granule loss. We spray it on site. No tear-off, no disposal fees, no week of disruption.

1
Penetrate
Polymer absorbs into the asphalt matrix, not just the surface.

2
Restore Flex
Replaces oils UV has dried out. Shingles regain elasticity.

3
Seal
Locks granules in place. Resists moisture infiltration.

The result is 10 to 15 additional years of useful service life on shingles that would otherwise hit replacement in 5 to 7. Treatment is non-toxic, dries within hours, and does not change the appearance of your roof.
