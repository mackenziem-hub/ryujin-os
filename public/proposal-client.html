The System

Every Plus Ultra roof is a complete CertainTeed system.

Six components working together. Here's where each one goes and why it matters.

1
Waterproofing Underlayment
Self-adhering rubber membrane at eaves, valleys, and penetrations. Your first defense against ice dams and wind-driven rain.
2
Synthetic Underlayment
Slip-resistant, tear-resistant water barrier across the full roof deck. Breathable secondary protection that outlasts old tar paper.
3
Starter Shingles
Installed along eaves and rakes. Creates a watertight seal at the roof edge and prevents shingle uplift in high wind.
4
Architectural Shingles
CertainTeed Landmark, Landmark Pro, or Grand Manor. Algae-resistant granules, reinforced fiberglass, Class A fire, 110 mph wind-rated.
5
Hip & Ridge Caps
Specialized shingles along the peaks and hips. Protects high-stress areas and gives the roof its finished look.
6
Ridge Vent & Ventilation
Ridge vents paired with intake vents keep the attic cooler in summer and drier in winter. Proper venting is the single biggest factor in how long your shingles actually last.
Also every tier, every roof: drip edge flashing, step flashings at walls, chimney saddle + counter-flashing, pipe boots, re-nailed sheathing with rotten-wood allowance (first 2 sheets included — beyond that quoted at $85/sheet and approved first), magnetic cleanup and full debris haul.
