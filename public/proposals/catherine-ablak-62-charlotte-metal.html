What you are buying

Why metal costs more.

The honest read

The panels themselves are a premium material and the install is a specialized trade. What you are buying is time.

A standing seam roof is very likely the last roof this house ever needs, where asphalt is a 30 year decision. For a steep, high-visibility roof like yours the metal lines also carry the curb appeal further than shingle does.
