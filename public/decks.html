Concepts.

The folder for every ultraslide we build: finance deep-dives, ad reviews, postmortems, sales playbooks, SOPs, and process maps. Each one is a single page on the shared slideshow engine: arrow keys or swipe to present, S for scroll mode, F for fullscreen, and N to drop a sticky note. No Gamma account, no export, the link lives right here.

To add a concept: ask Claude ("make an ultraslide on X"). It writes the deck on the engine, files it in Concepts, deploys it, and gives you the live link. Customer-facing roofing proposals are a separate tool, the Custom Scope Proposal Generator in Administration.
