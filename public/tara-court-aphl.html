Section 04 · Technology

What GoNano actually does

GoNano is a nanosilica treatment that modifies the molecular structure of asphalt shingles. It bonds to and within each shingle, then links shingles together at the microscopic level, restoring flexibility and creating a hydrophobic protective layer. Featured on CBC's Dragon's Den. Applied across Canada by certified dealers.

Impact resistance — Class 1 → Class 4 in two treatments

Independent UL testing (steel-ball drop test from 20 feet) confirms one GoNano treatment lifts a standard Class 1 shingle to Class 3 impact resistance. A second treatment achieves Class 4 — the highest possible rating and a documented insurance benefit.

Section 09 · Warranty & Protection

What's protected, for how long, by whom

FORTIFY SECTIONS

15-year non-prorated

Full coverage for the entire 15-year term. No reduction in value over time. Issued by GoNano directly.

REVIVE SECTIONS

10-year transferable

Full coverage for 10 years and the warranty transfers to a new owner if the property is sold. Adds resale value.

REPLACED SECTIONS

Plus Ultra 10-year workmanship + 15-year CertainTeed SureStart

Standard Plus Ultra labour warranty + CertainTeed manufacturer warranty on any new shingles. Stackable with Fortify treatment for further extension.

What's NOT covered (any scenario)

  • Acts of God beyond product specifications (Class 4 covers up to 2" hail; larger is outside spec)
  • Damage from third-party work (other contractors, satellite installers, etc.) after Plus Ultra leaves
  • Pre-existing structural deficiencies not identified during inspection (unlikely at Tara Court, but standard exclusion)
  • Routine periodic cleaning or maintenance — APHL retains responsibility for keeping gutters clear and debris off the roof
About manufacturer warranties GoNano treatments are documented not to void existing shingle manufacturer warranties. Where Tara Court has any remaining CertainTeed or other manufacturer coverage, treatment preserves that coverage in place.
Section 10 · Timeline & Logistics

How this lands on the property

Project sequencing (Option B)

  1. Week 1 — Authorization + deposit. Kevin signs Option B, APHL transfers 30% deposit. Plus Ultra schedules the work window and orders any replacement shingles.
  2. Week 2 — Cleaning prep. Plus Ultra crew sweeps, softwash where needed on all eligible sections. Photo-documented in CompanyCam.
  3. Week 3 — Building 3 replacement. Full tear-off + re-roof of the worst section. 3-4 day window. Tenants notified 48 hours in advance.
  4. Week 3-4 — GoNano application. NanoSeal-certified application across all rejuvenation-eligible sections. Single-day per building. No tear-off, no debris, no tenant disruption.
  5. Week 4 — Inspection sign-off. Plus Ultra + NanoSeal + Kevin walkthrough. Final balance due upon completion.
  6. Ongoing — Warranty records. APHL receives the warranty certificates from GoNano + the workmanship warranty from Plus Ultra. We file copies for ourselves.

Tenant impact

  • Replacement work (Bldg 3 only): Standard re-roofing noise during business hours. 3-4 days. Tenants notified in advance.
  • GoNano application: Minimal. Single-day per building, no tear-off, no shingle dust. Tenants likely won't notice the work happened.
  • Parking: One dumpster on-site for the replacement only. No long-term equipment storage.

Weather constraints

GoNano application requires dry shingles, no active precipitation, surface temperature above 5°C. NB roofing season runs roughly May-October. We'd target a June-July application window for optimal cure conditions.
