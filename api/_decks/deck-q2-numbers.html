08 / 09
How we know

Receipts, not estimates

  • Authoritative source: Stripe payment receipts to info@reply.plusultra-roofing.com, "you just received a total of CA$X against [address] Roof Invoice". That is the real all-in amount paid.
  • Deposit math: Plus Ultra deposits are exactly 30% of the all-in, so a deposit receipt back-solves the full contract (deposit / 0.30).
  • Excluded: QuickBooks / Atlantic Roofing invoices are subcontractor costs we owe, not revenue. They are kept out of these totals.
  • Known gap: Supabase estimates undercount signed revenue, which is why this is reconciled against receipts, not pulled from the system.
  • Next pass: cross-check against the bank statements to reconcile cash collected against signed jobs. Receipts can miss an e-transfer or cheque, so the bank record is the final ground truth for what actually came in.
